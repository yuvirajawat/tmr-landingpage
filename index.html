Why attend The Media Rumble ?

Media

From news to cinema, the forum will explore issues the media has grappled with in the last one year. Our media sessions include key subjects ranging from the Afghanistan crisis, Pegasus project and the rise of creators.

Technology

Tech is central to the rapid digital transformation taking place in media and the world. TMR 2021 will discuss the overlap between tech and media, use of technology for sustainable business models, it's conflicts and opportunities.

Policy

Policy governing media and platforms have been key news subjects this year. The forum will discuss the implications of the new IT rules, internet governance, climate negotiations and how the world sees China.
